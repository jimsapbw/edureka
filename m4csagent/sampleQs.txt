Here are the types of questions to test each intent:

FAQ Questions (handled by LLM with knowledge base):

"How do I reset my password?"
"How can I set up my account?"
"Where can I view my billing history?"
"How do I upgrade to premium features?"
"How do I contact support?"
"What payment methods do you accept?"
History Questions (uses personalized memory):

"What did I ask you before?"
"Can you remind me what we discussed last time?"
"Show me my previous queries"
"What issues have I reported?"
Complex/High-Risk Questions (escalated to human support):
These contain keywords: error, not working, complaint, refund, charge dispute, escalate, manager, lawsuit

"This feature is not working properly"
"I want to file a complaint"
"I need a refund for my subscription"
"There's an error when I try to login"
"I want to escalate this to a manager"
"There's a charge dispute on my account"
Fallback Questions (unclear intent):

"Tell me a joke"
"What's the weather like?"
"Can you help me with my homework?"
